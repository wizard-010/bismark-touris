About Bismark Touris — Premium Papua New Guinea Travel Experts

Our Story — Founded in Kokopo, East New Britain

Bismark Touris was founded in 2024 by David Bismark, a passionate advocate for premium tourism in Papua New Guinea. Born and raised in Kokopo, David grew up exploring the volcanic landscapes, swimming in the pristine waters, and learning the rich cultural traditions of the Tolai people. After years of working in luxury hospitality across the Pacific, he returned home with a vision: to create a premium tour company that would share the authentic beauty of East New Britain with discerning travelers from around the world.

What started as a one-man operation with a single vehicle has grown into a trusted team of experienced local guides, drivers, and cultural experts. We've welcomed travelers from Australia, New Zealand, Europe, North America, and Asia—each one leaving with stories, photos, and often, friendships that last a lifetime. Book your premium PNG adventure with the local experts who know this land best.

Our Mission — Authentic, Sustainable, Unforgettable Travel

At Bismark Touris, our mission is simple: to provide authentic, sustainable, and unforgettable premium travel experiences that connect visitors with the heart and soul of Papua New Guinea. We believe tourism should benefit local communities, preserve cultural heritage, and protect the natural environment for generations to come.

Every tour we operate is designed with these values in mind. We partner with local businesses, employ local guides, and contribute to community initiatives like school visits and cultural preservation projects. When you travel with us, you're not just a tourist—you're a partner in sustainable development. Choose Bismark Touris for responsible luxury travel in Papua New Guinea.
