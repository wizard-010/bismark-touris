About Bismark Touris — Premium Travel Agency Papua New Guinea

Our Story

Bismark Touris was founded in 2024 with a singular vision: to create the premier travel experience company in Papua New Guinea, specializing in luxury volcano tours, cultural expeditions, and authentic adventures in East New Britain Province. Born from a deep passion for sharing the volcanic landscapes, pristine waters, and rich cultural traditions of the region, our company has grown into the most trusted name in PNG tourism.

What began as a specialized tour operation has evolved into a comprehensive travel agency serving discerning travelers from Australia, New Zealand, Europe, North America, and Asia. Each guest leaves with stories, photographs, and connections that last a lifetime. Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition as the leading tour operator in East New Britain.

Our Mission

At Bismark Touris, our mission is to provide authentic, sustainable, and unforgettable luxury travel experiences that connect visitors with the heart and soul of Papua New Guinea. We believe premium tourism should benefit local communities, preserve cultural heritage, and protect the natural environment for generations to come.

Every premium tour we operate is designed with these values at its core. We partner exclusively with local businesses, employ experienced local guides, and contribute meaningfully to community initiatives including school visits and cultural preservation projects. When you travel with us, you're not just a guest—you're a partner in sustainable development and cultural preservation.
