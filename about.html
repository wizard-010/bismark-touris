About Bismark Touris — #1 Premium Travel Agency in Papua New Guinea

Bismark Touris is the leading premium travel agency in Papua New Guinea, specializing in luxury volcano tours, cultural expeditions, and authentic adventure experiences in East New Britain Province. As the most trusted tour operator in the region, we've welcomed discerning travelers from Australia, New Zealand, Europe, North America, and Asia since our founding in 2024.

Our Story: Excellence in PNG Tourism Since 2024

Bismark Touris was founded with a singular vision: to create Papua New Guinea's premier travel experience company. Our founders, born and raised in East New Britain, grew up exploring the volcanic landscapes of Mt Tavurvur, swimming in the pristine waters of Kokopo, and learning the rich cultural traditions of the Tolai people. After years of experience in the Pacific hospitality industry, they returned home to establish a travel agency that would showcase the authentic beauty of Papua New Guinea to the world.

What began as a specialized tour operation with a focus on quality over quantity has evolved into the #1 rated premium travel agency in East New Britain. Today, we operate a fleet of modern vehicles, employ a team of expert local guides, and offer comprehensive travel services including luxury volcano tours, cultural expeditions, and custom adventure packages. Every guest who travels with us leaves with stories, photographs, and friendships that last a lifetime.

Our Mission: Authentic, Sustainable, Unforgettable Travel

At Bismark Touris, our mission is to provide authentic, sustainable, and unforgettable luxury travel experiences that connect visitors with the heart and soul of Papua New Guinea. We believe premium tourism should benefit local communities, preserve cultural heritage, and protect the natural environment for generations to come.

Every tour we operate is designed with these values at its core. We partner exclusively with local businesses, employ experienced local guides, and contribute meaningfully to community initiatives including school visits, cultural preservation projects, and environmental conservation efforts. When you book with Bismark Touris, you're not just a guest—you're a partner in sustainable development and cultural preservation.
