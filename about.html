About Bismark Touris

Discover the story behind Papua New Guinea's trusted travel partner.

Our Story

Bismark Touris was founded in 2024 with a simple but powerful vision: to share the breathtaking beauty and rich cultural heritage of the East New Britain Province and Papua New Guinea with the world. Named after the majestic Bismarck Sea that borders our beautiful country, we are a locally-owned and operated tour company based in Kokopo, East New Britain Province.

Our founder, David, grew up exploring the volcanic landscapes, pristine beaches, and vibrant cultural traditions of East New Britain. After years of working in the tourism industry and witnessing visitors fall in love with his homeland, he decided to create a tour company that would offer authentic, responsible, and unforgettable travel experiences.

What started as a small operation with just a handful of local guides has now grown into a trusted name in East New Britain tourism. Today, we proudly serve hundreds of travelers each year, from cruise ship passengers seeking day tours to adventure travelers looking for immersive cultural experiences.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, authentic travel experiences that connect visitors with the natural beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality of Papua New Guinea, while supporting local communities and preserving our heritage for future generations.

Our Commitment to Responsible Tourism

At Bismark Touris, we believe that tourism should be a force for good. That's why we've built our business around principles of sustainability and community empowerment. We regularly visit local schools to donate supplies, employ local guides and drivers, partner with family-owned accommodations, and minimize our environmental footprint wherever possible.

We are proud members of the Papua New Guinea Tourism Promotion Authority and adhere to all national guidelines for responsible tourism practices.
