About Bismark Touris

Our Story

Bismark Touris was founded in 2024 by a passionate advocate for tourism in Papua New Guinea. Born and raised in Kokopo, our founder grew up exploring the volcanic landscapes, swimming in the pristine waters, and learning the rich cultural traditions of the Tolai people. After years of working in hospitality across the Pacific, he returned home with a vision: to create a tour company that would share the authentic beauty of East New Britain with the world.

What started as a one-man operation with a single vehicle has grown into a trusted team of experienced local guides, drivers, and cultural experts. We've welcomed travelers from Australia, New Zealand, Europe, North America, and Asia—each one leaving with stories, photos, and often, friendships that last a lifetime.

Our Mission

At Bismark Touris, our mission is simple: to provide authentic, sustainable, and unforgettable travel experiences that connect visitors with the heart and soul of Papua New Guinea. We believe tourism should benefit local communities, preserve cultural heritage, and protect the natural environment for generations to come.

Every tour we operate is designed with these values in mind. We partner with local businesses, employ local guides, and contribute to community initiatives like school visits and cultural preservation projects. When you travel with us, you're not just a tourist—you're a partner in sustainable development.

Our Values

Authenticity

We share real experiences, not tourist traps. Our tours take you to places that matter, with guides who live and breathe this land.

Sustainability

We minimize environmental impact, support local businesses, and invest in community development projects.

Community

We give back through school visits, donations, and cultural preservation initiatives. Tourism should benefit everyone.

Safety

Your safety is our priority. All guides are trained in first aid, and our vehicles are regularly maintained.

Why Choose Bismark Touris?

When you book with Bismark Touris, you're choosing more than just a tour—you're choosing an experience shaped by local knowledge, genuine hospitality, and a deep commitment to quality. Our guides are storytellers, historians, and friends who will help you see East New Britain through the eyes of those who call it home.

We're proud of the reviews we've received, the friendships we've formed, and the impact we're making in our community. Whether you're hiking an active volcano, exploring WWII tunnels, or sharing a meal with a local family, we'll be there to ensure your journey is safe, meaningful, and unforgettable.
