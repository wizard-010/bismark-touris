Mt Tavurvur Volcano Tours: Hike an Active Volcano in Papua New Guinea

Experience the thrill of standing on the rim of an active volcano. Book the best-guided volcano tours in Rabaul with expert local guides.

The Ultimate Volcano Trekking Experience in Papua New Guinea

For adventure travelers seeking the world's most dramatic volcanic landscapes, Mt Tavurvur volcano tours offer an unforgettable experience. Located in Rabaul, East New Britain Province, this active volcano is one of Papua New Guinea's most accessible and spectacular volcanic sites. Our guided volcano hikes take you to the crater rim, where you'll witness steaming vents, sulfur deposits, and panoramic views of the Rabaul caldera.

Whether you're a seasoned trekker or a first-time volcano visitor, our volcano trekking PNG tours cater to all fitness levels. With small group sizes, expert local guides, and a focus on safety, we provide the best Mt Tavurvur hiking experience in East New Britain.

Why Book Your Volcano Tour With Us?

Our guides are born and raised in the shadow of Mt Tavurvur. They know the best volcano trekking routes, the safest paths, and the fascinating stories that bring the Rabaul volcano to life.

Safety First - Volcanic Activity Monitoring

We monitor volcanic activity levels daily through the Rabaul Volcanological Observatory. Your safety is our priority—tours only operate when conditions are safe.

Best Volcano Photography Locations

Our guides know the best photography spots for capturing steam vents, sunrise over the caldera, and dramatic volcanic landscapes.

Safe Volcano Trekking in Papua New Guinea

All volcano tours are conducted with strict safety protocols. We maintain direct communication with the Rabaul Volcanological Observatory and monitor volcanic activity levels daily.

What to Bring on Your Volcano Adventure

  • Sturdy hiking boots – the volcanic ash trail requires good grip
  • Lightweight, breathable clothing – long pants recommended for ash protection
  • Sun hat, sunglasses, and high-SPF sunscreen
  • 2 liters of water per person
  • Camera with dust protection
  • Light rain jacket
