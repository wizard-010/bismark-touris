The Ultimate Volcano Trekking Experience in Papua New Guinea

For adventure travelers seeking the world's most dramatic volcanic landscapes, Mt Tavurvur volcano tours offer an unforgettable experience. Located in Rabaul, East New Britain Province, this active volcano is one of Papua New Guinea's most accessible and spectacular volcanic sites. Our guided volcano hikes take you to the crater rim, where you'll witness steaming vents, sulfur deposits, and panoramic views of the Rabaul caldera.

Whether you're a seasoned trekker or a first-time volcano visitor, our volcano trekking PNG tours cater to all fitness levels. With small group sizes, expert local guides, and a focus on safety, we provide the best Mt Tavurvur hiking experience in East New Britain. What to Bring on Your Volcano Adventure

  • Sturdy hiking boots – the volcanic ash trail requires good grip
  • Lightweight, breathable clothing – long pants recommended for ash protection
  • Sun hat, sunglasses, and high-SPF sunscreen – the tropical sun is intense at elevation
  • 2 liters of water per person – staying hydrated is essential
  • Camera with dust protection – volcanic ash can be abrasive
  • Light rain jacket – weather can change quickly in the Highlands
  • Small backpack to carry your personal items

Frequently Asked Questions About Volcano Tours

Is Mt Tavurvur currently active and safe to visit?

Yes, Mt Tavurvur remains active with continuous steam emissions. We monitor volcanic activity levels daily through the Rabaul Volcanological Observatory. Tours only operate when conditions are safe for visitors.

How difficult is the Mt Tavurvur hike?

The volcano trek is rated moderate. The trail has some steep sections and loose volcanic ash. A reasonable level of fitness is recommended. We're happy to discuss your fitness level before booking.

What is the best time of year for volcano tours?

The dry season (May-October) offers the best conditions for volcano hiking. However, Mt Tavurvur tours operate year-round. Morning departures are recommended for cooler temperatures and better visibility.

Do I need travel insurance for volcano trekking?

Travel insurance covering adventure activities is strongly recommended for all our volcano tours. This should include coverage for emergency evacuation, medical expenses, and trip cancellation.
