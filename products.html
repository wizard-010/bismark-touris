Discover the best of East New Britain with our carefully curated experiences. Each tour is led by knowledgeable local guides who bring the history, culture, and natural beauty of Papua New Guinea to life.

Duration: Half Day (3-4 hours) | Difficulty: Easy

Visit the world-renowned Rabaul Volcanological Observatory, where scientists monitor the region's active volcanoes. Learn about the instruments used to predict eruptions and enjoy panoramic views of Simpson Harbour and Tavurvur.

  • Observatory tour with scientist talk (subject to availability)
  • Malmaluan Lookout viewpoint stop

WWII History Tours

Japanese Tunnel Exploration

Duration: Half Day (3-4 hours) | Difficulty: Easy

Descend into the network of tunnels carved by Japanese soldiers during WWII. These underground passages served as command centers, hospitals, and storage facilities. Your guide will share stories of wartime Rabaul and the tunnel's strategic importance.

  • Flashlight and safety gear provided
  • Historical context and photographs
  • Visit to multiple tunnel sites
  • Transportation included

Price: From PGK 250 per person

Bitapaka War Cemetery & Heritage Tour

Duration: Half Day (3 hours) | Difficulty: Easy

Pay respects at the beautifully maintained Bitapaka War Cemetery, where soldiers from both World Wars are laid to rest. Learn about the Pacific campaign and the role Rabaul played in one of history's most significant conflicts.

  • Guided cemetery tour with historical context
  • Visit to nearby coastal defense guns
  • Reflection time and photography
  • Expert local historian guide

Price: From PGK 280 per person
