Experience the breathtaking volcanoes, rich WWII history, and warm Melanesian culture of East New Britain Province with Bismark Touris.

Welcome to Bismark Touris

Based in Kokopo, East New Britain Province, Bismark Touris is your gateway to one of Papua New Guinea's most spectacular regions. Founded in 2024 by local tourism expert David Bismark, our company is built on a passion for sharing the natural wonders and cultural richness of our homeland.

From the rumbling Mt. Tavurvur volcano to the serene Bitapaka War Cemetery, from vibrant local markets to hidden hot springs—we offer authentic experiences that connect you with the heart of Papua New Guinea. Our team of experienced local guides ensures every journey is safe, informative, and unforgettable.
