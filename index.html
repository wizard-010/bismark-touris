Welcome to Bismark Touris — Premier Travel Agency in Papua New Guinea

Based in Kokopo, East New Britain Province, Bismark Touris is the leading provider of luxury volcano tours, private cultural expeditions, and bespoke travel experiences in one of the world's most spectacular regions. Founded in 2024, our company is built on a passion for sharing the natural wonders and cultural richness of our homeland with discerning travelers seeking authentic, high-end adventures.

From the rumbling Mt. Tavurvur volcano — one of the most accessible active volcanoes on earth — to the serene Bitapaka War Cemetery, from vibrant local markets to hidden geothermal hot springs, we offer premium experiences that connect you with the heart of Papua New Guinea. Our team of experienced local guides ensures every journey is safe, informative, and unforgettable. Rated #1 tour operator in East New Britain by international travelers.
