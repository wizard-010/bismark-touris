Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please call or WhatsApp us directly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (PGK), bank transfer, and mobile banking. For international travelers, we can arrange secure payment options.

Do you offer tours in other languages?

Yes! We offer tours in English, German, and French. Please specify your language preference when booking.

Are tours accessible?

We can accommodate most mobility needs. Please let us know in advance so we can arrange appropriate transportation.
