Melanesian Tourist Services: A Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1972, Melanesian Tourist Services has been at the forefront of Papua New Guinea's tourism industry for over 50 years. Based at Madang Resort, the company has built an enviable reputation for quality, reliability, and exceptional service. Their fleet ranges from small motorboats for intimate excursions to luxury yachts capable of extended cruises, all maintained to the highest standards.

What sets MTS apart is their local knowledge. Their captains and crew have spent their lives navigating the waters of Madang and beyond, knowing every hidden reef, every secluded beach, and every village that welcomes visitors. When travelers book with MTS, they gain access to generations of maritime expertise and genuine Melanesian hospitality.

The Fleet: Vessels for Every Adventure

Melanesian Tourist Services maintains a diverse fleet designed to cater to different group sizes and experiences:

Luxury Yacht Charters

For those seeking the ultimate marine experience, MTS offers luxury yacht charters with experienced captains and crew. These vessels are equipped with comfortable seating, shaded decks, refreshment facilities, and premium sound systems. Perfect for corporate events, special celebrations, or simply indulging in a day of luxury on the water.

Island Cruisers

Their fleet of island cruisers are ideal for day trips to Madang's famous offshore islands. Spacious and comfortable, these vessels accommodate groups of 6 to 20 passengers, with ample space for diving equipment, picnic supplies, and camera gear.

Speedboats

For smaller groups seeking adventure, MTS's modern speedboats offer the perfect combination of speed and comfort. Ideal for reaching remote dive sites, visiting multiple islands in a single day, or simply enjoying the thrill of skimming across the Bismarck Sea.

Dive Boats

Specially equipped for diving expeditions, these vessels feature tank racks, camera tables, and easy entry points for divers. MTS has partnered with some of the world's leading dive operators, offering access to Madang's world-renowned dive sites.

Signature Boat Tours

Kalibobo Island & Village Experience

No visit to Madang is complete without a trip to Kalibobo Island, the iconic landmark that graces Madang's waterfront. MTS offers half-day tours to this picturesque island, where visitors can swim, snorkel, and explore the village. The tour includes a traditional welcome, cultural demonstrations, and the opportunity to purchase local crafts directly from the community.

Duration: 3-4 hours
Includes: Boat transfer, village visit, snorkeling equipment, refreshments

Coral Reef Discovery Tour

Madang's coral reefs are among the most biodiverse in the world, with an extraordinary array of hard and soft corals, reef fish, and marine life. This half-day tour takes guests to some of the most accessible and spectacular reefs, with opportunities for snorkeling and photography. Guides introduce visitors to the reef's inhabitants, from clownfish to turtles, and explain the importance of coral conservation.

Duration: 4-5 hours
Includes: Boat transfer, snorkel gear, guide, refreshments, lunch

Bismarck Sea Sunset Cruise

Experience the magic of a Madang sunset from the water. This leisurely cruise takes guests along the coastline as the sun sinks into the Bismarck Sea, painting the sky in shades of orange, pink, and gold. Includes sparkling wine and canapés, making it the perfect romantic outing or celebration.

Duration: 2 hours
Includes: Boat transfer, sparkling wine, canapés, sunset viewing

Full-Day Island Hopping Adventure

Discover the beauty of Madang's offshore islands on this comprehensive day tour. Visit multiple islands, each with its own character—from the historic Kalibobo to the pristine beaches of Kranket and the bird-rich forests of Wongat. Includes a beachside lunch, snorkeling at multiple sites, and plenty of time for swimming and relaxation.

Duration: 7-8 hours
Includes: Boat transfer, island visits, beach lunch, snorkel gear, guide, refreshments

Private Charter – Custom Itinerary

For those who want to create their own adventure, MTS offers private charters with custom itineraries. Whether guests are a diving group wanting to reach specific sites, a family seeking a secluded beach picnic, or a photographer chasing the perfect light, the experienced team will design a day that matches their vision.

Duration: Flexible
Includes: Private boat and captain, custom itinerary, crew, refreshments (additional activities available)

World-Class Diving from Madang

Madang is widely recognized as one of the world's premier diving destinations, with over 1,500 species of fish and an extraordinary diversity of coral. Melanesian Tourist Services partners with Madang's leading dive operators to offer a complete diving experience:

  • Reef Dives – Access to over 40 dive sites within 30 minutes of Madang, including the famous Magic Passage, known for its incredible coral gardens and pelagic encounters.
  • Wreck Dives – Explore WWII wrecks scattered throughout Madang's waters, including Japanese freighters and aircraft.
  • Night Dives – Experience the reef after dark, when nocturnal species emerge and coral polyps display their feeding tentacles.
  • Liveaboard Expeditions – For serious divers, MTS can arrange extended liveaboard trips to remote sites along the north coast.

All diving packages include experienced guides, equipment rental, and safety briefings. Certification required for dive excursions; introductory dives available for beginners.

Combining Madang Boat Tours with Goroka Show 2026

September is an excellent time to visit Madang, with calm seas and clear visibility for diving and snorkeling. The Goroka Show in mid-September offers a perfect opportunity to combine coastal luxury with highlands culture.

Planning Your Madang Luxury Boat Experience

Best Time to Visit

The dry season (May through October) offers the most favorable conditions for boat tours and diving in Madang. September is particularly ideal—calm seas, excellent visibility, and the opportunity to combine with the Goroka Show. Water temperatures average 28-30°C (82-86°F) year-round.

Getting There

Madang is accessible via daily flights from Port Moresby with Air Niugini and PNG Air. Flight time is approximately one hour. From the airport, Madang Resort and Melanesian Tourist Services are a short drive away. All transfers can be arranged as part of the package.

Accommodation

Melanesian Tourist Services is based at Madang Resort, the town's premier accommodation. The resort offers comfortable rooms with ocean views, a swimming pool, restaurant, and bar. All boat tours depart directly from the resort's waterfront. Alternative accommodation options in Madang include Madang Lodge and the Madang Hotel.

What to Bring

  • Swimsuit and towel – For swimming, snorkeling, and beach visits
  • Sunscreen and sun hat – The tropical sun is intense on the water
  • Camera with waterproof housing – For underwater photography
  • Snorkel gear – Provided, but personal equipment welcome
  • Lightweight clothing – Comfortable for tropical temperatures
  • Windbreaker or light jacket – Boat rides can be breezy
  • Waterproof bag – For protecting electronics and valuables
  • Dive certification card – If planning to dive
  • Cash – For purchasing crafts and souvenirs

Conservation and Sustainable Tourism

Melanesian Tourist Services is committed to sustainable marine tourism practices. Their captains and crew are trained in responsible boating practices, including maintaining safe distances from marine life, proper waste management, and anchoring only in designated areas to protect coral. When visitors tour with MTS, they support a company that has been a steward of Madang's marine environment for over 50 years.

All visitors are encouraged to practice responsible snorkeling and diving: never touch coral, maintain neutral buoyancy, and avoid feeding fish. These simple practices ensure that Madang's reefs remain healthy for generations to come.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book boat tours in advance?

Yes, especially during peak season (May-October) and for private charters. Booking at least 2-3 months in advance is recommended to secure preferred dates and vessels. For Goroka Show combinations, book by June 2026.

Are boat tours suitable for children?

Absolutely. MTS offers family-friendly tours with life jackets for all ages. The island hopping and reef discovery tours are particularly popular with families. Children under 12 receive discounted rates.

What if the weather is bad?

Safety is the top priority. If conditions are unsafe, tours will be rescheduled or refunded. Guides monitor weather forecasts and keep guests informed.

Can I combine diving with other activities?

Yes. Many tours combine diving or snorkeling with island visits and cultural experiences. Custom itineraries can be designed to maximize time on the water.

What is the cost of a Madang luxury boat tour?

Prices vary based on tour type, duration, group size, and vessel choice. Half-day tours start from PGK 350 per person. Private charters start from PGK 1,800 for a half-day (up to 6 people). Contact Bismark Touris for a personalized quote including Goroka Show combinations.
